Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Modern Furniture Gallery located?

We are located at 7025 Tomken Rd #19, Mississauga, ON L5S 1R6, easily accessible from Brampton, Toronto, Oakville, and across the GTA.

How long does it take to make custom furniture?

Most custom pieces are completed within 1–3 weeks depending on the design and fabric selected. Contact us for a more precise timeline on your specific order.

Can I choose my own fabric and colour?

Absolutely. Every piece is fully customizable. Visit our Mississauga showroom to browse our full selection of fabrics, leathers, and colours — or contact us to discuss your vision.

Do you offer wholesaler pricing to the public?

Yes! We sell directly to the public at wholesaler prices with no retail markup. You get factory-direct quality Canadian-made furniture at the best prices in the GTA.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of the Greater Toronto Area including Mississauga, Brampton, Toronto, Etobicoke, Oakville, Vaughan, and surrounding areas.
